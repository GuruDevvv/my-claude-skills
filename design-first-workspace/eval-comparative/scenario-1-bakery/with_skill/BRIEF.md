# Design Brief: "Мука и Время" Landing Page

## Project
Landing page for craft bakery "Мука и Время" — custom cakes, sourdough bread, pastries.

## Audience
Women 28-45, arriving from Instagram, mobile-first usage.

## Desired Emotion
Warmth, craft, homemade feel, trust, appetite.

## Primary Action
Order a custom cake (form submission), browse menu.

## Brand Direction
- Name: "Мука и Время"
- Aesthetic: Craft, artisan, handmade
- No existing brand assets — building from scratch

## Content
- Headlines: "Мука и Время" / "Выпечка с душой" / "Каждый торт — история"
- CTA: "Заказать торт"
- Sections: Hero, menu preview, order form

## Anti-references
- NOT generic food delivery
- NOT corporate
- NOT fast-food aesthetic

## Technical Constraints
- Vanilla HTML/CSS
- Mobile-first responsive
- Google Fonts CDN only (Cyrillic-safe)
